Introducing Just Looking, an Audio-Visual Diary by Rohan Misra and Sam Ratcliffe. The act of filming is an inherently mindful exercise. It forces you to slow down and experience what’s happening in front of you, even if it’s nothing of any particular consequence. Just Looking is an audiovisual diary comprised of moments and spaces that we have collected throughout our time here, occasionally using audio pairing to utilise the link between music and memory in our brains. We encourage you to stop what you’re doing and enjoy the pleasure of just looking 🧘‍♂️
