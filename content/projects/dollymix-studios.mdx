Dollymix Studios is a streetwear brand founded in 2018. We recently reimagined the visual identity of the brand to realign with the founders tastes. The Hasbro Logo from 1978 was the original reference for this project. This was referenced as gaming manufactures often have mature and simple visual identities, but produce games that inspire fun and excitement every single day. In this context, Dollymix facilitates play' in the form of providing the garments which are fairly simplistic but therefore allow people to create in every day.
