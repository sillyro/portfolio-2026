MintStars is a web3 startup in the private creator space. They hired us to update their branding assets to feel more bright, modern and trusting. We began by changing typeface to make it more recognisable and also embraced lowercasing throughout their whole brand identity. We reimagined the colour palette from pastel shades to more saturated, contrasting tones to improve brand memorability.
