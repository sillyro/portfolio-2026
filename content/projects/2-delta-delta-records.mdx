2 Delta Delta is a UK Garage & Breakbeat record label based in London. Founded by DJ/Producer, Dylan Biggs [DRB Selects], I built a brand identity strategy for both the label and the founder.

## Deliverables

- **Logos** — Wordmark, monogram, and lockups for digital and print.
- **Album art** — Cover templates and release visuals across the roster.
- **SoundCloud banner** — Profile header and campaign graphics for streaming.
