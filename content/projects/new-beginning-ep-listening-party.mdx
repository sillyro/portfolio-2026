Dollymix Studios presents: New Beginning EP Listening Party Sillyro’s musical influences are a product of the music that him and his friends love listening to together, so in the road to the release of his debut EP we hosted a listening party at the studio where Dollymix was born. Five unique sets from friends were captured over the course of the evening, reflecting sounds that be heard on the EP, ranging from vinyl house grooves to euphoric breaks. The project is currently live and set videos are released weekly throughout April in the build up to the EP launch.

## Deliverables

- Event organisation & management 
- Full set videos (YouTube)
- Short form clips (Instagram / Tiktok)
- Set audio (SoundCloud)
